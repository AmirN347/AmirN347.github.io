Everyone deserves the chance to learn the fun part of mathematics

Martices is a realling interesting topic in mathematics that highschoolers dont get to learn and college students despise. Without further specifications, matrices represent linear maps, and allow explicit computations in linear algebra. Therefore, the study of matrices is a large part of linear algebra, and most properties and operations of abstract linear algebra can be expressed in terms of matrices. For example, matrix multiplication represents composition of linear maps.
